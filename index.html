About me....

I am a person who chooses to be happy and joyful. No need for conflict unless it's used for a valid tool for growth and experience. I am an artist who choose dance and coding as my medium. I love music and its inspiring qualities. My motto is Trust, Communication, and Respect. Everything else stems from those. I am a welcoming person. Acknowledge the energy within every molecule which makes me to live my life smoothly (but spunky), energetically (but with room for relaxation), courageous, and perceptive.

My pursuit in coding has both a professional and personal goal. Professionaly, I would like to be part of this ever growing field. Technology is no longer a field only for computer science majors and mathematicians but is a part of our everyday life. I would like to be apart of this new renaissance. Help make the world a little better for everyone.

On a personal level I would love to get into combining technology and dance. Specifically with drones. I want to create dance pieces were the dancer and drone dance organically to together. Something like Cirque du Solei did. I constantly hear how technology is destroying human interaction. But I believe it can bring us closer together. I hope to express that through combining my two favorite things.
